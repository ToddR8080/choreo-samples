AirportShare - Terms of Use

About AirportShare

ACI World AirportShare API Platform (referred hereafter as “AirportShare”) is an API publishing platform for the aviation industry.

Terms of Use

This section explains the terms of use for the AirportShare platform.

Data Providers

ACI World and any and all Data Providers that choose to provide data to ACI World for the AirportShare API Platform must agree to a set of terms and conditions of use for the platform. Please contact ACI World for more details.

Data Consumers

Any and all Data Consumers that wish to have access to the AirportShare API Platform shall sign a data license agreement with ACI World, as provided by ACI World, prior to getting access to such data. More details can be obtained by contacting ACI World.

Privacy Policy

This section explains how AirportShare captures your personal information, purpose of capturing, and the retention of your personal information.

What is personal information ?

AirportShare considers anything related to you as your personal information. This includes, but is not limited to,

Your user name The IP address you use to login Your device ID, if you choose to login with a device (Phone, Tablet)

However AirportShare does not consider the following as your personal information, and uses this only for analytical purposes, since this information cannot be used to track you.

City/Country from which your TCP/IP connection originates Time of the day you login.(Year, Month, Week, Hour or Minute) Type of the device you use to login (Phone, Tablet, etc.) Operating system and Generic browser information

Collection of your information

AirportShare collects your information to only serve your access requirements.

For example, AirportShare uses your IP address to detect any suspicious login attempt to your account. AirportShare uses your First Name and Last Name to provide rich and personalized information.

Tracking Technologies

AirportShare collects your information through the following, The user sign up page where you enter your personal data Tracking your IP address with HTTP request, HTTP headers, and TCP/IP Tracking your geographic information with the IP address Your login history with browser cookies. Please refer our cookie policy for more information

Use of your personal information

AirportShare will use your personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected (or for a use identified as consistent with that purpose).

AirportShare uses your personal information only for the following purposes. To provide you with a personalized user experience. AirportShare uses attributes such as your name for this purpose To protect your account from unauthorized access or a potential hacking attempt. AirportShare use HTTP or TCP/IP Headers for this purpose This includes, IP address, Browser fingerprinting, Cookies To derive statistical data for analytical purposes on system performance improvements. AirportShare will not keep any personal information after statistical calculations. Thus a statistical report has no means to identify an individual person AirportShare may use The IP Address to derive geographic information Browser fingerprinting to determine the browser technology and version

Disclosure of your personal information

AirportShare will disclose personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected (or for a use identified as consistent with that purpose), unless you have consented otherwise or where it is required by law.

Legal process

AirportShare may disclose your personal information with or without your consent where it is required by law following the due and lawful process.

How AirportShare keeps your personal information

Where your personal information is stored

AirportShare stores your personal information in secured databases. AirportShare exercises proper industry accepted security measures to protect the database where your personal information is held.

AirportShare may use encryption to keep your personal data with added level of security.

How long does AirportShare keep your personal information ?

AirportShare keep your personal data as long as you are an active user of our system. You can update your personal data at any time with the given self-care user portals.

How can you request a removal of your personal information ?

You can request the administrator to delete your account The administrator will be the administrator of the tenant you are registered or the super-administrator if you do not use the tenant feature.

You can additionally request to anonymize all traces of your activities that may have been retained by AirportShare in Logs, Databases or Analytical storage.

Changes to this policy

Upgraded versions of AirportShare may contain changes to this policy and revisions to this policy will be packaged within such upgrades. Such changes would only apply to users who choose to use upgraded versions.

The organization running AirportShare may revise the Privacy Policy from time to time. You can find the most recent governing policy with the respective link provided by the organization running AirportShare. The organization will notify any changes to the privacy policy over our official public channels.

Your choices

If you already have an account with AirportShare; you have the right to deactivate your account if you find that this privacy policy is unacceptable for you.

If you do not have an account, you can choose not to subscribe if you do not agree with our privacy policy.
